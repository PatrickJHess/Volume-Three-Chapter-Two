# Clean Prices, Dirty Prices, and Accrued Interest




<p style="font-family: 'Garamond', serif; font-size: 16px; text-indent: 0.25in; line-height: 1.6;">
This chapter focused on a key calculation in bond pricing: <em>Accrued Interest</em>. Several new functions were developed and deployed. Although introduced to illustrate the calculation of accrued interest, they are destined for heavy lifting in future chapters. The <em>"Iceberg Principle"</em> was identified as a major advantage of Python's abundant libraries and modules. This principle is a highlight of our approach and a key feature for effective learning.
</p>
